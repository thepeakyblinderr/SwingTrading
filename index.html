📖 Momentum Swing Trading — Beginner's Playbook
1
Find the Trend
Price must be above its 20 EMA and 50 EMA on the daily chart. Never buy a falling stock — trade with the trend, not against it.
2
Check RSI Momentum
RSI (14) between 55–70 is the sweet spot. Above 70 = overbought (risk of reversal). Below 50 = weak momentum. Avoid both extremes.
3
Confirm with Volume
Volume should be 1.5x+ the 20-day average on the breakout day. High volume = smart money participation. Low volume breakouts often fail.
4
Set Entry, SL & Target
Buy on breakout or next day open. SL below recent swing low (3–5 days). Target = 2× to 3× the SL distance (minimum 1:2 R:R). Use the Risk Calculator.
5
Trail & Exit Discipline
Once up 50% of target, trail SL to breakeven. Exit at target OR if RSI crosses below 50 OR price closes below 20 EMA. Never hold losers hoping for recovery.
⚠️ Rules for ₹25K Capital
📌 Risk max ₹500 per trade (2% of capital). This lets you survive 10+ losing trades in a row.
✅ Max 2 open trades at once. Don't over-diversify with ₹25K — focus!
❌ Avoid stocks above ₹2,000/share — you can't get adequate quantity.
🎯 Best price range: ₹50–₹800/share for meaningful quantity with ₹25K.
⏱️ Hold period: 5–15 trading days. Review every Friday.
📅 Market timings: NSE 9:15 AM – 3:30 PM IST. Best entry: 9:30–10:30 AM or 2:00–3:00 PM.
